# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Please keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on the project.

Examples of expected behavior:
- be direct without being hostile
- assume good faith unless there is strong evidence otherwise
- keep issues and pull requests on topic
- describe problems precisely
- accept that maintainers may close requests that do not fit the project

## Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- doxxing or publication of private information
- spam or repeated off-topic promotion
- abusive or manipulative behavior toward users, contributors, or maintainers

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that is abusive, unsafe, or clearly out of scope.
